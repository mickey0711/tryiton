Refund Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

💡 We want you to be completely satisfied with TryIt4U AI. If you're not happy, we'll make it right.

1. Subscription Refunds

We offer refunds under the following conditions:

  • Within 14 days of purchase: Full refund, no questions asked — for any new paid subscription.
  • After 14 days: Refunds are evaluated on a case-by-case basis. Please contact our support team.
  • Annual subscriptions: Pro-rated refund available within 30 days of purchase for the unused portion.

2. Credit Packs

AI Try-On credit packs are non-refundable once credits have been used. Unused credits within a credit pack may be refunded within 14 days of purchase.

3. Free Plan

The Free plan is provided at no cost and is not eligible for refunds.

4. Exceptions — When We Do Not Refund

  • Refund requests made after 14 days of the initial purchase (monthly) or 30 days (annual), unless there is a proven technical failure on our end.
  • Accounts terminated due to violations of our Terms of Service.
  • Credits that have already been consumed.

5. Service Failure Refunds

If our AI service is unavailable for more than 24 consecutive hours during your billing period due to issues on our end, you are entitled to a pro-rated credit or refund for the affected period. We will proactively notify you and apply credits automatically.

6. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us:

  • Email: support@tryit4u.ai
  • Subject line: "Refund Request — [your account email]"
  • Include your order/transaction ID if available.

We aim to process all refund requests within 3–5 business days.

7. Cancellations

You may cancel your subscription at any time from your Account Settings. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period — you retain access to all features until then. We do not charge cancellation fees.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. The latest version is always available at tryit4u.ai/refund.
